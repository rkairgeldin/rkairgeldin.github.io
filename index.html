Research interests

My research focus is on Machine Learning and Optimization, specifically tree-based models and neural network compression. My recent projects are on the topic of tree-based models in various domains: supervised learning, clustering, neural network compression, vector quantization and model interpretability.

About

I am a 4th year PhD candidate at University of California, Merced (UCM) supervised by Miguel Á. Carreira-Perpiñán. I hold a MSc in Computer Engineering and BEng in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from NU in Astana, Kazakhstan. You can find my CV [here] .
